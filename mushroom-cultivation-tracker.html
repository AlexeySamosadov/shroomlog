🍄ShroomLog

A mushroom cultivation tracker built on real grow data

Free · private · offline · no account required

Good cultivation is a feedback loop: change one variable, measure the result, keep what works. But that loop only exists if you write things down. ShroomLog is a cultivation tracker that captures the numbers that actually move your yield — how long colonization took, when pins appeared, how much each flush weighed, and where contamination crept in — across every grow you run.

The full cultivation cycle, on one timeline

  • Inoculate — record the species, substrate (CVG, masters mix, straw…) and method (monotub, grow bag, jar) and the start date.
  • Colonize — log a colonization percentage as the mycelium spreads, so you can see how fast a given species runs.
  • Fruit & pin — mark the move to fruiting conditions and the first pins so you learn your real timelines.
  • Harvest — weigh every flush in grams; total yield is summed automatically per grow.
  • Review — compare grows and species to find what consistently produces.

Why grams matter

"It did pretty well" is not data. Recording harvest weight per flush is the single highest-value habit a home cultivator can build — it tells you which substrate, species and conditions are worth repeating. ShroomLog makes weighing a flush a five-second log entry, then does the math for you so the comparison is there whenever you want it.

Contamination is a lesson, not just a loss

Every contaminated tub is information. Track the contamination type and ShroomLog builds a picture of your clean rate and your most common failure modes over time, so you can adjust technique — better sterilization, fresh air exchange, drier substrate — with evidence behind the change instead of folklore.

Private by design

ShroomLog runs entirely in your browser. Your grows are stored locally, never uploaded, and the app works with no internet connection — ideal for a basement or grow tent. Unlimited grows, full stage tracking, yield totals and complete history are free. A one-time Pro unlock adds CSV export, per-species yield statistics, deeper contamination analysis and side-by-side grow comparison for the data-driven grower.
